Intranet

Definition

An intranet is a private network used within an organization to share information, resources, and services securely among its members.

Key Features

  • Private and restricted access
  • Used inside organizations (schools, companies, hospitals)
  • Shares files, announcements, and internal systems
  • More secure than the public internet

Intranet vs Internet

  • Internet: Public, anyone can access it
  • Intranet: Private, only authorized users can access it

Example

A company may use an intranet for employees to access payroll systems, internal emails, and company documents.
