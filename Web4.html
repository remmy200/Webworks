Web 4.0

Definition

Web 4.0 is a proposed future stage of the World Wide Web where communication between humans and machines becomes highly intelligent, seamless, and real-time, often described as the “symbiotic web.”

Key Characteristics

  • Highly intelligent systems using AI and automation
  • Real-time interaction between humans and machines
  • Seamless integration of physical and digital worlds (IoT)
  • Personalised and predictive services

How It May Work

Web 4.0 systems could automatically understand what a user wants, predict needs, and complete tasks without requiring manual input, using advanced AI and connected devices.

Examples (Future Ideas)

  • Smart homes that fully automate daily routines
  • AI systems that manage your schedule and decisions
  • Fully connected smart cities

Key Point

Web 4.0 is known as the “symbiotic and intelligent web”.
